Performed quantitative microCT analysis of murine tibiae to investigate the effects of taurine and leptin treatment on bone structural adaptation. Analysis included cortical and trabecular bone phenotyping, validation of BoneJ-derived measurements against ScanCo outputs, normalization of tibial scan lengths, and generation of Python-based visualizations for structural metrics including cortical thickness, bone volume, cross-sectional area, and moments of inertia.
