---

Performed microCT-based analysis of mechanically loaded mouse tibiae to investigate the role of Slc13a5 in osteoblast-mediated bone remodeling and marrow fat regulation. Quantified cortical and trabecular adaptation, analyzed osmium-stained marrow adiposity datasets, and interpreted structural changes associated with altered Slc13a5 signaling. Findings contributed to a symposium poster presentation and ongoing manuscript preparation.
